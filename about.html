Who we are

Our Passion

At Olde Bridge Restaurant we have over half a century of combined experience under one roof. Locals will recognise Bhupender's warm welcome the moment they first enter, and head chef Raj is renowned for a long career cooking the finest Indian and Thai food.

We are truly focused on quality — which is why you will never find frozen meats in our kitchen. Only the freshest herbs and spices call our kitchen home.

Situated on Westport's iconic Bridge Street, we pride ourselves on serving among the finest Indian & Thai dishes in the West. We also offer a few European favourites — because sometimes only a Prime Sirloin Steak will do.
